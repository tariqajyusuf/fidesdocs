# VCDPA: What is it and how to comply?

Following California’s CPRA, The Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (CDPA or VCDPA) is the second privacy law to go into effect in 2023. If your business operates in Virginia or targets its products or services to Virginia residents, you’ll need to comply with Virginia’s privacy law.

This guide will walk you through everything you need to do to in a step-by-step guide to comply with Virginia’s CDPA.
