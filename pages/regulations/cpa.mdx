# CPA: What is it and how to comply?

The Colorado Privacy Act (CPA) will go into effect on July 1, 2023. This will be the third U.S. state privacy law to go into effect this year, following the [California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA)](https://ethyca.com/docs/regulations/ccpa) and the [Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA)](https://ethyca.com/docs/regulations/vcdpa) .  

If your business operates in Colorado or targets products or services to Colorado residents, you’ll need to comply with CPA. This guide will give you a step-by-step walkthrough of how Ethyca can help you comply with Colorado’s privacy law.
